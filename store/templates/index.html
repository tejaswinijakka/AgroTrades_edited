About Us

We are Students of CBIT Hyderabad with our project AgroTrades

This web based application is a package mainly for farmers who are living in the vicious cycle of middleman and also for vendors as well as retailers who wants to buy food essentials from anywhere to find good quality and price. This application:

  • It gives complete information about what crop to grow, depending upon land condition and weather conditions.
  • It provides a platform for farmers to eliminate middlemen.
  • It can be used by both farmers as well as retailers/consumers accordingly.
